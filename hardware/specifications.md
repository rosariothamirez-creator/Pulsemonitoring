## Hardware Specifications

### 1. Digital Accelerometer
* **Model:** ADXL345 (3-Axis Digital Accelerometer Module)
* **Measurement Range:** Selectable ±2g, ±4g, ±8g, or ±16g
* **Resolution:** Up to 13-bit (3.9 mg/LSB)
* **Output Data Rate (Sampling Rate):** Configurable from 0.1 Hz up to 3200 Hz
* **Communication Protocol:** I2C / SPI

### 2. Microcontroller
* **Model:** Raspberry Pi Pico W
* **Processor:** Dual-core Arm Cortex-M0+ (running up to 133 MHz)
* **Memory:** 264 KB on-chip SRAM and 2 MB onboard Flash memory
* **Wireless Connectivity:** 2.4GHz Wi-Fi (802.11n)
* **Operating Environment:** Bare-metal (programmed via MicroPython / C++ SDK)

### 3. Storage Module
* **Model:** Micro SD Card Module (Standard SPI Interface)
* **Communication Protocol:** SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface)
* **Supported Card Types:** Micro SD / Micro SDHC
* **Operating Voltage:** 5V/3.3V compatible (includes onboard 3.3V level shifter)

### 4. Power Supply Unit (PSU)
* **Model:** Mean Well RS-15-5 (Industrial Switching Power Supply)
* **Input Voltage:** 85 ~ 264 VAC / 120 ~ 370 VDC
* **Output Voltage:** 5 VDC
* **Max Output Current:** 3 A
* **Rated Power:** 15 W

### 5. Enclosure
* **Material:** PLA (Polylactic Acid)
* **Manufacturing Method:** FDM 3D Printing
* **Purpose:** Component protection, structural integrity, and portability
